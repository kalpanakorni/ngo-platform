Our story

Founded on one idea: help should never stop at the first hand.

Ripple began in 2011 as a small volunteer network responding to flood relief in three villages. Today we run education, medical, and disaster-response programs across 14 regions — but the model hasn't changed: every donation is tracked to the person or place it reaches, and every volunteer hour compounds into something bigger than any one gift.

Mission

Close the gap between people who can give and people who need help — quickly, transparently, and with dignity on both sides.

Vision

A world where emergency aid, education, and healthcare reach every community within days, not years, of being needed.
